Riding My Emotional Horse

Programming seems to trigger me often, into a state of amused frustration, where I am laughing at myself and the weird errors that I have no idea how I've generated but also frustrated because I don't even know what I need to fix. I guess the solution to that is to try to amplify the amused part until the frustration disappears faster and faster.



The Siberian North Railroad technique is pretty effective, especially just the first part I'm sure everyone has come across the whole "just take a deep breath", so its nothing revolutionary but it still works. It's quite effective as something to imagine, a train quickly getting stuck in snow.
